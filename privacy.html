Privacy Policy

Clippy ("App") converts your local videos, generates subtitles, and if you connect TikTok, uploads clips to your TikTok account using the official API.

Data We Process

  • Local video/audio files you select (processed on your device).
  • Generated subtitle files (stored locally unless you delete them).
  • TikTok OAuth tokens (stored only on your device to authenticate uploads).

What We Don’t Collect

No analytics, advertising identifiers, or server side storage by the creator. All data remains local unless you choose to upload to TikTok.

Sharing

When you choose to upload, the App sends your selected video to TikTok via their API after you grant permission.

Retention & Deletion

Tokens are stored locally and can be removed by disconnecting in the App or deleting the token file. Media and subtitles remain on your device until you delete them.

Your Rights

Contact us to request data assistance or ask questions.
