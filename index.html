Features

9:16 conversion

Centers your video on a portrait canvas (no crop), with color or blurred background.

TikTok segments

Split output into 1–30 minute segments; name and queue clips automatically.

Auto subtitles

Local speech‑to‑text to create SRT; optionally burn‑in styled subtitles.

Upload to TikTok

After you connect TikTok via OAuth, the app uploads clips using the official API.

Local processing

All processing happens on your device; no creator servers store your data.

Queue & speed

Batch create multiple clips with GPU/CPU‑aware encoding for fast exports.

Contact

Questions or support? Send a message and it will open your email client.
